# Rapid-Prototyping-with-APP-Inventor
This course is organized by St. Joseph's Education for  Excellence (SEE) an innovative venture by St. Joseph's HSS Peravoor in association with  Vimal Jyothi Engineering College, Chemperi, Kannur. During the course, students will gain hands-on experience developing Android applications. Prototypes are interactive models of ideas that are in the early stages of development. App Inventor is used for rapid app prototyping. The course is open to students with any level of computing experience and does not require prior programming knowledge.
